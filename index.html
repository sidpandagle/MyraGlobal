The Myra Global Rice Collection

Discover our curated selection of premium Basmati rice, each variety processed to perfection to meet the highest international standards.

1121 Golden Sella Basmati

A parboiled marvel, the 1121 Golden Sella is celebrated for its exceptional grain length (avg. 8.35mm), which doubles upon cooking without sticking. Its golden hue and nutty flavor make it the king of biryanis and elaborate rice dishes.

Technical Specifications:

  • Moisture: 12-13%
  • Broken: < 1%
  • Purity: 95%
  • Avg. Grain Length: 8.35 mm

Packaging Solutions:

Available in branded non-woven & PP bags: 1kg, 5kg, 10kg, 25kg, 50kg.

Pusa Steam Basmati

Processed through a unique steaming technique, this variety retains more nutrients. It cooks into long, slender, and fluffy grains with a delicate aroma, making it a versatile choice for a wide range of international cuisines.

Technical Specifications:

  • Moisture: 12-13%
  • Broken: < 2%
  • Purity: 94%
  • Avg. Grain Length: 7.45 mm

Packaging Solutions:

Available in branded non-woven & PP bags: 1kg, 5kg, 10kg, 25kg, 50kg.
